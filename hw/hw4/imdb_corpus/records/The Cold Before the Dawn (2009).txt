The Cold Before the Dawn (2009)

In The Cold before the Dawn we follow Sammy, a young man whose profession 
is unknown. The film begins with his discovery of a dead prostitute, Mia, 
who he was once in a relationship with. Sammy returns to his home, visibly 
shaken by his discovery. He is visited by Val, a friend of Mia, who asks 
Sammy to help find Mia's killer. She is convinced that a man called 'Heavy 
Breath' is involved and suggests that he visit Mia's brother, a drug dealer 
called Banks. Sammy visits Banks but is warned not to continue his 
investigation. Ignoring this, Sammy breaks in to Mia's flat and finds her 
diary, which reveals that Heavy Breath was a drug dealer who systematically 
drugged and raped her, eventually killing her when she tried to run away. 
Sammy meets with Val, passing on the diary to her. As she walks away, she 
is confronted by a figure in a gas mask. Val gives the diary to the figure, 
who attacks Sammy and reveals himself as Banks. Sammy is confronted with 
the horrific truth than Mia was raped and killed by her own, psychopathic 
brother. Sammy is killed by Banks and the terrible secret is buried.
